The History

Calcio Storico is believed to have started in the 1500s with its origins being connected to the Roman game “Harpastum”. The game was first played in Florence by the city’s aristocratic class as a form of entertainment and competition; however, it wasn't until the 1900s that the game became the city’s official sport (Insidecom Editorial Staff)& (Flores). Under the rule of Benito Mussolini, Italy was attempting to bring back its old traditions and become a unique and cultured nation. In 1930 the first city wide Calcio game was organized and from then on, was permanently embedded into the city’s traditions. (SABELLA)

The Competition

The ancient Florentines played Calcio to pass time but also as a form of training for soldiers. The modern Calcio however, is played as a form of  entertainment and a method to unite the city (Insidecom Editorial Staff). The teams are based on the districts of Florence;  Santa Croce-blue, Santo Spirito-white, Santa Maria Novella-red, and San Giovanni-green. The prize for winning is, traditionally, a Chinnian calf (Stefan), however, nowadays the prize for the winning team is a free meal at a fancy restaurant.

The Rules

                As mentioned before, there are four teams that participate in the city wide sport. Each team consists of twenty seven players, or “Calciante”, and each match the teams play lasts fifty minutes. The matches are played amongst two randomly chosen teams; and in order to win a team must have the higher score by the end of the match. To attain points, players must either score a goal by getting the leather ball into the fenced area on the opposite side of the arena, or by tackling the opposing player who is carrying the ball (Flores). In the ancient version of the sport there were very minimal rules regarding the safety of players, however, nowadays the sport has strict rules that prioritize the safety of its participants. The modern rules consist of, only one on one fights are permitted unless the opposing player carries the ball, no kicks or sucker punches to the head, and all ligaments may be used in fights. Additionally, the modern sport has implemented the use of Referees and coaches in the same ways of other active sports today. (Stefan)

The Effect

The city of Florence was founded by the Roman army in the 1st century Bce along with the game of Calcio. As mentioned before, Calcio originated from the Roman game Harpastum, and was played as a form of training for the Roman legionnaires. Furthermore the game immediately impacted the culture of Florence and would continue to do so through its use as a neighborhood sport. Nowadays the sport is a key part of the rich Florentine culture; it brings revenue to the city as a tourist attraction and the game unites the districts of the city. (Netflix)
